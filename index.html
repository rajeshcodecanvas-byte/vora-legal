Privacy Policy for Vora

Last updated: April 2024

At Vora: Smart Unit Converter, we prioritize your privacy. This policy explains our commitment to data security and transparency.

1. Data Collection

Vora is a no-cloud, offline-first application. We do not collect, store, or share any personal identity data, real-time location data, or contact information. Your conversion history and favorite units are stored strictly locally on your device's private storage.

2. Third-Party Services

Vora does not use any third-party analytics, advertisements, or tracking software. Your conversion data never leaves your smartphone and is never shared with any external servers.

3. Children's Privacy

Vora does not knowingly collect any personal information from children. As an offline utility, it is designed to be safe for all users, including students and children of all ages. We comply with COPPA and global privacy standards by ensuring zero data transmission.

4. Data Control & Deletion

Users have complete control over their data. You can permanently clear all stored local preferences and history at any time using the "Clear All Data" button within the app's About/Settings menu. Uninstalling the app will also permanently remove all local data.
