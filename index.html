Privacy Policy for Vora

Last updated: April 2026

At Vora, we prioritize your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your information.

1. Data Collection

Vora is a no-cloud application. We do not collect, store, or share any personal data, location data, or contact information. All conversion history and favorite units are stored locally on your device using Android DataStore.

2. Third-Party Services

Vora does not use any third-party analytics or tracking software. Your data never leaves your phone.

3. Data Deletion

Users can clear all stored data at any time using the "Clear All Data" button within the app's About menu.
